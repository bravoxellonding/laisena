Key Research Findings

The Creator Economy Shift

Our longitudinal study of 5,000+ video creators shows that AI-assisted workflows lead to a 3.2x increase in content output without compromising quality. However, success increasingly depends on strategic thinking and brand development rather than technical editing skills alone.

Read full report →

Labor Market Dynamics

While automation displaces certain routine editing tasks, it simultaneously creates demand for new roles: AI prompt engineers, creative directors for AI systems, and ethics specialists. Net job creation in the creative sector is projected at +18% by 2030.

Read full report →

Accessibility & Inclusion

AI tools lower geographic and economic barriers to professional video production. Creators in emerging markets now have access to capabilities previously limited to well-funded studios, fostering a more diverse global creative ecosystem.
